THE PAMOJA LARGE SCALE PIGGERY PROJECT

Pigs are like gold hidden in mud. Let's show you our calculations supported by factual informations of how pigs are actually hidden gold. This large scale project which we can start small can get us out of poverty.

In 2 Years, we are projected to reach 1500 heads of pigs annually (explanation bellow)

In 5 years, we expected to have a large farm with over 10,000 pigs

In uganda a pig is at $218, so in 5 years we expect a net worth of over $2.1 millions USD

In 10 years from now, we are projecting to produce 100,000 pigs annually.

100,000 pigs annually means we will have an annual gross income of $21.8m USD

At this point, each of our 50 members can have a share of arounnd $300,000 annually
